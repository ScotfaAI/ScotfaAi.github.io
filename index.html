Exploring the Intersection of AI and Microscopy at the University of Warwick

About Me

I am a PhD student in Interdisciplinary Biomedical Sciences at the University of Warwick, focusing on the innovative integration of artificial intelligence with microscopy. My work aims to automate complex imaging processes, enhancing research capabilities and accuracy in biological studies.

I work with a variety of flourescence microscopy methods, particularly the Spinning Disk Confocal and Lattice LightSheet.

Open To Work
